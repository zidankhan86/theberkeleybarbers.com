Why do we have a thousand clients a month?

Since our founding in 1955, the motto has been the same, even as we have grown and expanded, and with a generation of new barbers joining us. We are proud to call our shop “Old School,” because we adhere to the same values since we first opened our doors:

Our clients matter

We want you comfortable when you visit us. We listen to you as you tell us how you want your hair cut or styled – whether the same haircut as before or a new look.

We want you 100% satisfied

each time. Which is why we have more than 1000 clients who visit us regularly. We have clients whose hair we’ve been doing for decades – and now we are doing their kids!

We have a NO-attitude vibe

Our barbers are talented, lowkey, professional and heart-based. Our clients can relax and chill in a friendly environment that treats every client as important – and it’s not just PR spin. Every client matters.
